## Pre-Launch Validation Checklist

### Phase 1: Strategy Validation

#### Event Definition
- [ ] **Event date and time finalized** - Include multiple timezones (PT, ET minimum)
  - Reference: "Aug 16, 2025 and starts at 9am Pacific / 12pm Eastern" (Source: Registration_Page_Content.md, line 13)
- [ ] **Platform confirmed** - YouTube Live, Zoom, or other
  - Reference: "I will stream it on Youtube Live. No fancy tech." (Source: Registration_Page_Content.md, line 13)
- [ ] **Core promise documented** - What registrants will get from the event
- [ ] **Mystery element planned** - Something to be revealed live that creates curiosity
  - Reference: "something else I've quietly worked on for over a year" (Source: Registration_Page_Content.md, line 13)
- [ ] **Registration goal set** - Target number documented
  - Reference: "Our goal is to pass 1,000,000+ registrations by launch day" (Source: Registration_Page_Content.md, line 13)

#### Funnel Architecture
- [ ] **Registration-to-revenue funnel mapped**
  - Registration Opt-in → VIP Sales Page → Checkout → Thank You → Event
  - Reference: "Opt-in de Registro → Pagina de Vendas VIP (VSL) → Pagina de Checkout → Pagina de obrigado" (Source: Main_Offer_Landing_Page.md, lines 106-115)
- [ ] **VIP ascension path designed** - Benefits stack documented
- [ ] **Post-registration sequence planned** - All emails and SMS mapped

### Phase 2: Copy Validation

#### Registration Page Copy

##### Hook/Opening
- [ ] **Immediate invitation clear** - Makes the ask right away
  - Template: "Quick question. Can I get your email address? Because I want to invite you to the biggest [EVENT] of the [SEASON]."
  - Reference: "Pergunta rapida. Posso pegar seu endereco de email?" (Source: Pre_Event_Registration_VSL.md, line 5)
- [ ] **Investment/effort mentioned** - Shows skin in the game
  - Reference: "Eu gastei mais de 6 milhoes de dolares neste evento" (Source: Pre_Event_Registration_VSL.md, line 5)

##### Authority Statement
- [ ] **Company/portfolio mentioned** - Establishes credibility
- [ ] **Specific numbers included** - Results must be quantifiable
  - Reference: "empresas do portfolio que fizeram mais de 250 milhoes de dolares no ano passado" (Source: Pre_Event_Registration_VSL.md, line 9)
- [ ] **Personal accomplishment included** - Builds trust
  - Reference: "eu vendi tres empresas em 2021" (Source: Pre_Event_Registration_VSL.md, line 9)

##### Value Framework
- [ ] **Clear framework presented** - What they'll learn
  - Reference: "Todo negocio tem quatro objetivos principais em termos de ganhar dinheiro" (Source: Pre_Event_Registration_VSL.md, lines 11-13)
- [ ] **Event content previewed** - Specific topics mentioned
- [ ] **Excitement built** - "It's going to be insane"

##### Mystery Element
- [ ] **Secret/surprise teased** - Creates irresistible curiosity
- [ ] **Comparison framework used** - "Better than X, less than Y"
  - Reference: "e melhor que um NFT e custa menos que um Bitcoin" (Source: Pre_Event_Registration_VSL.md, line 17)
- [ ] **Live attendance required** - Must show up to get it
  - Reference: "cada pessoa que aparecer ao vivo...recebera um absolutamente gratis" (Source: Pre_Event_Registration_VSL.md, line 17)
- [ ] **Mystery is REAL** - Will actually be delivered at event

##### CTA
- [ ] **Action feels effortless** - One simple step
  - Template: "All you have to do is ONE thing: Enter your email below and click submit. That's it."
  - Reference: "Voce se inscreve abaixo e clica em enviar. E isso" (Source: Pre_Event_Registration_VSL.md, lines 20-21)
- [ ] **VIP upgrade mentioned** - Teases the upsell
  - Reference: "Voce pode fazer upgrade para VIP e literalmente e tudo gratis" (Source: Pre_Event_Registration_VSL.md, line 22)

##### Social Proof
- [ ] **Registration count displayed** - Real numbers
  - Reference: "Ja temos mais de 300.000 pessoas registradas" (Source: Pre_Event_Registration_VSL.md, line 24)
- [ ] **Projection included** - Shows momentum
  - Reference: "Provavelmente serao cerca de 500.000 quando o evento comecar" (Source: Pre_Event_Registration_VSL.md, line 25)
- [ ] **Numbers are real and updated** - Never fake

#### Confirmation Email Copy
- [ ] **Subject line: "You're registered. (Save this email)"**
  - Reference: Registration_Page_Content.md, line 12
- [ ] **Congratulations + event name included**
- [ ] **"PLEASE SAVE THIS EMAIL" warning prominent**
  - Reference: "PLEASE SAVE THIS EMAIL. IT HAS IMPORTANT BOOK LAUNCH DETAILS." (Source: Registration_Page_Content.md, line 13)
- [ ] **Event details complete:**
  - [ ] Platform (YouTube Live, etc.)
  - [ ] Date with multiple timezones
  - [ ] Calendar link
- [ ] **Registration milestone mentioned** - Social proof
- [ ] **Mystery teased again** - Maintains curiosity
- [ ] **VIP upgrade mentioned**

#### SMS Confirmation Copy
- [ ] **Under 160 characters**
- [ ] **Includes name personalization**
- [ ] **Sets expectations** - How many more messages they'll receive
  - Template: "Hey {Name}, it's [CREATOR] - you'll get [X] more launch texts: [Y] reminders + [Z] goodies."
  - Reference: Registration_Page_Content.md, line 10
- [ ] **Opt-out instructions included** - "Reply 'Stop' to stop"
- [ ] **References email for details**

#### Nurture Sequence Copy

##### 15-Minute Follow-up
- [ ] **Subject: "in case you missed this"**
  - Reference: Registration_Page_Content.md, line 23
- [ ] **Confirms registration status** - "You're already registered"
- [ ] **VIP benefits stacked with checkmarks**
- [ ] **Clear CTA to upgrade**

##### 24-Hour Reminder
- [ ] **Subject: "Get your book first + VIP Livestream"**
  - Reference: Registration_Page_Content.md, line 36
- [ ] **Operational reason given** - Why pre-ordering helps
- [ ] **VIP benefits detailed**
- [ ] **"Same price" reminder** - No extra cost logic
- [ ] **Calendar link included**

##### 48-Hour Affiliate Invitation
- [ ] **Subject: "Win a day with me (free)"**
  - Reference: Registration_Page_Content.md, line 45
- [ ] **Previous launch stats included** - Social proof
  - Reference: "~32,000...brought 130,000+ people with them" (Source: Registration_Page_Content.md, line 46)
- [ ] **Simple explanation of referral process**
- [ ] **Prizes benefit BOTH referrer and friends**
- [ ] **Affiliate signup link included**

#### Countdown Sequence Copy
- [ ] **45-day announcement email ready**
- [ ] **Weekly content emails scheduled**
- [ ] **48-hour checklist email includes:**
  - [ ] Calendar reminder
  - [ ] VIP upgrade CTA
  - [ ] "Desktop is best" recommendation
  - [ ] Share with friend CTA
  - [ ] Registration count
  - [ ] Early join warning (12-17 minutes early)
    - Reference: "it is best to join 12-17 minutes early" (Source: Registration_Page_Content.md, line 218)
  - [ ] Full timezone list
- [ ] **24-hour final reminder ready**

#### Launch Day Sequence Copy
- [ ] **3-hour before email ready** (VIP and non-VIP versions)
- [ ] **60-minute before email ready**
- [ ] **15-minute "WE ARE LIVE" email ready** - Minimal copy, direct link
- [ ] **+15 minute email from different sender** - Re-engagement tactic
  - Reference: "It's Leila, I stole Alex's email" (Source: Registration_Page_Content.md, line 296)
- [ ] **During-event offer emails ready**

## Escalation Matrix

| Issue | Escalate To | Timeline | Action |
|-------|-------------|----------|--------|
| Form not working | Tech Lead | 15 min | Fix or switch to backup |
| Emails not delivering | Email Platform + Dev | 1 hour | Check domain, switch sender |
| Registration drop >30% | Marketing Lead | Same day | Audit page, check traffic |
| VIP conversion <1% | Offer Owner | 24 hours | Review offer, adjust copy |
| Mass unsubscribes | Marketing Lead | Immediately | Pause campaign, investigate |
| Negative feedback | Customer Success | 2 hours | Respond, address concern |
| Page down | DevOps | 15 min | Restore from backup |
| SMS not delivering | SMS Platform | 30 min | Check carrier issues |
