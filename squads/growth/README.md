# Growth Squad

> Scaling, Optimization & Experimentation

## Overview

The Growth Squad focuses on scaling Travel Tech Digital through systematic experimentation, data analysis, and optimization. Responsible for:

- Growth strategy and waves
- A/B testing and experiments
- Funnel analysis and optimization
- Viral and referral mechanics
- Market and competitor research

## Growth vs Marketing

| Marketing | Growth |
|-----------|--------|
| Brand & messaging | Optimization & metrics |
| Content creation | A/B testing |
| Channel strategy | Funnel analysis |
| Positioning | Experimentation |

Growth takes Marketing inputs and optimizes for scale.
