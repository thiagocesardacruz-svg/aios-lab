## Common Issues to Prevent

### Email Issues
- ❌ Emails landing in spam
- ❌ Broken links
- ❌ Wrong personalization
- ❌ Sending at wrong times

### UX Issues
- ❌ Confusing first screen
- ❌ Too many steps required
- ❌ No clear next action
- ❌ Overwhelming new users

### Technical Issues
- ❌ Slow loading times
- ❌ Broken automation
- ❌ Missing tracking events
- ❌ Edge cases not handled
