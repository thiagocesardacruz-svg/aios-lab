## Onboarding Best Practices

### Do
- Start with a win
- Show progress (X of Y steps)
- Celebrate completions
- Provide escape hatches
- Follow up on drops

### Don't
- Overwhelm with options
- Hide important features
- Require everything upfront
- Ignore mobile users
- Skip personalization
