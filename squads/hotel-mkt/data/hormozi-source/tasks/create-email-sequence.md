## Purpose
Create a high-converting email sequence that nurtures leads toward purchase.

> "Email is owned media. You control it. No algorithm can take it away." — Alex Hormozi

---

## Email Sequence Types

### Type 1: Welcome Sequence (5-7 emails)
- Day 0: Deliver lead magnet + set expectations
- Day 1: Your story + why you do this
- Day 2: Quick win content
- Day 3: Common mistake to avoid
- Day 4: Case study / social proof
- Day 5: Soft pitch
- Day 6: Direct offer

### Type 2: Launch Sequence (7-10 emails)
- Pre-launch: Build anticipation (3 emails)
- Cart open: Announce + urgency (4 emails)
- Cart close: Final push (3 emails)

### Type 3: Nurture/Broadcast (ongoing)
- Value-first content
- Maintain give:ask ratio (3:1 minimum)
- Occasional offers

---

## Email Structure Formula

### Subject Line
- Curiosity + benefit
- 40 characters max
- Test with vs without personalization

### First Line
- Hook that makes them read line 2
- Question or bold statement
- No fluff

### Body
- One idea per email
- Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences)
- Conversational tone

### CTA
- Clear single action
- Link early and often
- PS reinforces offer

## Email Templates

### Welcome Email (Day 0)
```
Subject: Your [Lead Magnet Name] is here

Hey [Name],

Here's what you asked for: [Link]

Quick tip to get the most out of it:
[One actionable insight]

Tomorrow I'll send you [teaser for Day 1].

Talk soon,
[Signature]
```

### Story Email (Day 1)
```
Subject: Why I created [Lead Magnet]

[Name],

[X] years ago, I was struggling with [problem].

I tried everything:
- [Failed attempt 1]
- [Failed attempt 2]
- [Failed attempt 3]

Then I discovered [mechanism].

That's why I created [Lead Magnet] — so you don't
have to go through what I did.

Tomorrow, I'll share the #1 mistake people make
with [topic].

[Signature]
```

### Pitch Email
```
Subject: Quick question

[Name],

Do you want help with [problem]?

I just opened up [offer] for a limited time.

Here's what you get:
- [Benefit 1]
- [Benefit 2]
- [Benefit 3]

[Link to offer]

But heads up — [scarcity element].

[Signature]

PS - [Reinforce urgency or benefit]
```

## Execution Steps

### Step 1: Map the Sequence
- Determine sequence type
- Plan email topics for each day
- Define the end goal

### Step 2: Write Subject Lines First
- 5 options per email
- Test curiosity vs direct benefit
- Check length (40 chars)

### Step 3: Write Emails
- Hook → Body → CTA format
- One idea per email
- Read aloud to check tone

### Step 4: Add Links
- Track all links
- Use consistent CTA buttons
- Include PS with link

### Step 5: Set Up in Email Platform
- Schedule with proper timing
- Test all links
- Preview on mobile
