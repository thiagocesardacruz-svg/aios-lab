## Purpose
Create a high-converting landing page that captures leads with maximum efficiency.

> "A landing page has ONE job: get them to take the next step. Every word, image, and button exists to do that job." — Alex Hormozi

---

## Landing Page Formula

### Above the Fold (Critical 5 Elements)

1. **Headline** — Clear benefit, specific outcome
2. **Subheadline** — How they'll get it
3. **Hero Image/Video** — Result visualization
4. **Form/CTA** — Single clear action
5. **Proof Snippet** — One powerful testimonial or stat

### Below the Fold (Supporting Elements)

6. **3 Benefits** — What they get (icons + short text)
7. **Social Proof** — Logos, testimonials, numbers
8. **What's Included** — Bullet list of deliverables
9. **FAQ** — 3-5 top objections answered
10. **Final CTA** — Repeat the action

---

## Optimization Checklist

### Speed
- [ ] Page loads in under 3 seconds
- [ ] Images optimized
- [ ] No unnecessary scripts

### Mobile
- [ ] Fully responsive
- [ ] CTA visible without scrolling
- [ ] Form easy to complete on phone

### Clarity
- [ ] Can understand offer in 5 seconds
- [ ] Single CTA (no competing actions)
- [ ] No navigation menu (reduce exits)

### Trust
- [ ] Real testimonials with names/photos
- [ ] Privacy policy linked
- [ ] Secure form indicators

## Execution Steps

### Step 1: Define the One Action
What do you want them to do?
- Submit email for lead magnet
- Book a call
- Start free trial
- Register for event

### Step 2: Write the Headline
Formula: `Get [Specific Result] [Timeframe/Ease]`

Examples:
> "Get Your Free Sales Script Template"
> "Book Your Growth Audit (Takes 15 Minutes)"
> "Start Your 14-Day Free Trial"

### Step 3: Create the Form
Minimum fields only:
- Email (always)
- Name (if needed for personalization)
- Phone (only if sales call required)

### Step 4: Add Proof
Choose strongest proof type:
- Testimonial quote
- Number of customers
- Results achieved
- Press mentions
