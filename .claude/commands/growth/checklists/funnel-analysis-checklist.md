## Common Pitfalls

### Avoid These
- ❌ Analyzing without clear stage definitions
- ❌ Ignoring segment differences
- ❌ Single point-in-time analysis only
- ❌ No benchmark comparison
- ❌ Recommendations without data backing
- ❌ Focusing only on conversion, ignoring time

### Best Practices
- ✅ Start with clear hypotheses
- ✅ Segment early and often
- ✅ Compare to relevant benchmarks
- ✅ Quantify opportunities
- ✅ Prioritize by impact AND ease
- ✅ Document everything
